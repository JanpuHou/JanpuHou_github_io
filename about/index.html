About

My research interests are in Statistics and Machine Learning, including convolutional neural networks, interpretability and statistical inference. I was born and raised in Taiwan, graduated from National Cheng Kung University and got a Ph.D from Princeton University. Now work in fiber optics manufacturing field with research interests in Light Detection and Ranging(LiDAR). At my previous research position at Honeywell, I worked on MicroOptics Devices and Optical Sensors. I received Chairman's Technical Achievement Award as a member of the Backlight Team. I live in Las Vegas. In my free time I like to play golf, tennis and snorkeling.
